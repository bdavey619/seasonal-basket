July · weekday

Pasta

July tomatoes make the sauce — you don't need a jar.

Keep

  • Pasta
  • Olive oil and garlic

July adds

Start with
  • Heirloom tomatoes, roughly chopped
Choose from
  • Fresh basil

Variations

Tomatoes + basil This is probably the version you'll make most often.
Tomatoes + red pepper flakes If you want a little more from it.
Tomatoes + white beans + basil If there's no protein in the house.

Finish

Take the pan off the heat before the tomatoes go in — they need warmth, not cooking. Tear basil over the top last.

Works well with

  • Chicken thighs
  • Ground turkey
  • White beans
  • No added protein

From Field Notes

  • Add herbs after the heat
