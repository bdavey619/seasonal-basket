July · San Diego

Sweet corn

Corn sugars convert to starch quickly after picking. July corn eaten within a day or two of harvest is a different ingredient from corn that has traveled for a week. Grocery stores turn over fast in summer — the corn on the shelf right now is likely good.

How to choose it

  • Husk tightly wrapped and bright green
  • Silk slightly dry at the top, not slimy
  • Plump kernels felt through the husk
  • Avoid any ears with dried-out or blackened husks

Buy this much: 2–3 ears for one person mid-week, another 1–2 for the weekend

On a weekday

  • Cut off the cob into a rice bowl
  • Added to taco bowls with beans and tomato
  • Quick corn and tomato salad with basil

On a weekend

Corn salad alongside grilled chicken thighs and peaches.

One thing worth learning

Fresh corn is good raw. Shaved directly off the cob into a salad, it has a sweetness that disappears quickly with heat.

Good with this month

Heirloom tomatoes, Basil, Persian cucumbers, Peaches

Good with your staples

ground beef or turkey, chicken thighs, beans, sticky rice

Storage

Keep husked corn refrigerated and use within two days. Husked is fine for a day, unhusked is better if waiting.
