Persian cucumbers

Small, seedless, and thin-skinned, Persian cucumbers are at their crispest in summer. They need no peeling and almost no preparation.

How to choose it

  • Firm along the entire length
  • Deep, even green
  • No soft spots or wrinkling
  • Smaller is usually crisper

Buy this much: 4–6 cucumbers for one person for the week

Often one of the least expensive things in the basket. Sold in bags at most grocery stores — good value relative to how far they stretch across the week.

During the week

  • Rice bowl topping, sliced thin
  • Quick salad with tomato and basil
  • Side with salmon and mint
  • Wrap filling with chicken and yogurt

On a weekend

Smashed cucumber salad with sesame, chili crisp, and scallion.

One thing worth learning

Salt briefly before dressing and let the water draw out for a few minutes. The cucumber becomes more concentrated and holds its crunch longer.

Good with this month

Heirloom tomatoes, Basil, Mint, Sweet corn

Good with your staples

salmon, chicken thighs, beans, sticky rice, greek yogurt

Storage

Refrigerate in a loose bag. Use within one week. Best used early in the week.
