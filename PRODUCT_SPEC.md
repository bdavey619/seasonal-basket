# Seasonal — Product Specification

## Product Promise

Walk into the grocery store knowing exactly what to buy.

## Primary User Need

The user wants to eat seasonally but does not know:

- What is at its best right now
- What to buy
- How much to buy
- How to use it throughout the week
- How to combine seasonal ingredients with familiar staples

## Core User Behavior

The product should support two modes:

### Weekday mode

Fast, dependable, repeatable meals.

Examples:

- Sticky rice
- Ground beef or turkey
- Chicken thighs
- Salmon
- Beans
- Sourdough
- Greek yogurt

Seasonal adds produce, herbs, sauces, and flavor direction.

### Weekend mode

One or two meals that feel worth slowing down for.

Examples:

- Tomato, burrata, basil, and sourdough
- Grilled peaches
- Corn salad
- Farmers market lunch

## MVP Scope

### In scope

- One location: San Diego
- One edition: July
- Eight featured ingredients
- One static monthly page
- One page per featured ingredient
- One realistic weekly plan
- One confidence score
- One weekend meal
- One drink
- One thing to notice
- Contributor / guide module
- Seasonal color palette
- Botanical illustration placeholders

### Out of scope

- Authentication
- Grocery delivery
- Nutrition tracking
- Calorie tracking
- Search
- User-generated recipes
- Restaurant recommendations
- National expansion
- Dynamic personalization
- Payments
- Mobile app

## Visual Direction

The site should feel like:

- A field guide
- An almanac
- A trusted local publication
- A durable reference object

It should not feel like:

- A startup dashboard
- A recipe search engine
- A social feed
- A food influencer site
- A meal-prep app

## Monthly Color System

Colors are derived from the basket.

For July:

- Tomato red
- Basil green
- Burrata cream
- Peach
- Dark leafy green
- Warm paper background

Each month should have its own palette while preserving layout, typography, and system behavior.

## Long-Term Principle

The content should remain useful year after year.

The page may improve, but the edition should not depend on trends, news, or constant updates.
